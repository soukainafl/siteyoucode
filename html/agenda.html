CodeChallenge, powered by IBM Digital Nation Africa, is a hackathon organized by MACM (Moroccan Association for Computing Machinery) to bring innovative ideas that would solve issues related to Water, Education, Health and Agriculture. #CodeChallenge brings together university students with brilliant ideas from all over Morocco, giving them the opportunity to showcase their skills in IT and Mobile App development. Teams can participate in the challenge with a mobile application that addresses an issue in one of the fields chosen for this edition which are: water, health, education, and agriculture. The competition starts with the submission of project abstracts which will be evaluated by a Jury who will select 20 teams for the semi-finals. These teams will pitch their projects for 10 minutes on the first day of the event to advance to the final round which is reserved for the top ten teams. The qualified teams will receive mentoring from professionals before their 15-min final pitch. The finalists will have the opportunity to meet, exchange, and network with our specialist mentors and investors, enjoy the tasty snacks, and create an unforgettable experience with new friends. The winning team will receive a monetary prize of 20.000 Dh to help them take their project to the next level.
